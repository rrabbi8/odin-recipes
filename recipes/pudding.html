Caramel Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a heavy-bottomed saucepan, heat the sugar and water over medium heat, stirring until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Turn up the heat to high and continue to cook the sugar until it turns amber in color, about 5-7 minutes. Do not stir the sugar once it begins to boil.
  4. Pour the caramel into a 9-inch (23cm) baking dish and swirl to coat the bottom evenly. Set aside to cool.
  5. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract.
  6. Pour the egg mixture into the prepared baking dish over the caramel.
  7. Place the baking dish in a larger baking pan and fill it with water until it reaches halfway up the sides of the pudding dish.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes, or until the pudding is set and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Remove the pudding from the oven and let it cool to room temperature.
  10. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight, before serving.
  11. To serve, run a knife around the edges of the pudding to loosen it from the dish.
  12. Place a serving dish upside down over the baking dish and carefully flip it over.
  13. The caramel sauce will flow over the pudding, creating a delicious glaze.
  14. Slice and serve chilled. Enjoy!
