Chotpoti

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas, boiled
  • 2 cups puffed rice
  • 1/2 cup tamarind pulp
  • 1/2 cup boiled potatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro leaves, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the boiled chickpeas, diced potatoes, red onion, cilantro leaves, and green chili.
  2. Add the tamarind pulp and mix well.
  3. Season with roasted cumin powder, chaat masala, red chili powder, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  4. In a serving bowl, add the puffed rice.
  5. Top the puffed rice with the chickpea mixture.
  6. Garnish with more cilantro leaves and green chili.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
